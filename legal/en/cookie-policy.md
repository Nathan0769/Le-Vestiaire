# Cookie Policy

**Last updated**: November 2025

## What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies help us provide a better user experience and improve our services.

## Cookies Used on Le Vestiaire

### 1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the site to function and cannot be disabled.

- **Authentication cookies**: Allow you to log in and maintain your active session
- **cookieConsent**: Remembers your cookie preferences

### 2. Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use the site to improve our services.

- **Vercel Analytics**: Traffic analysis and user behavior tracking
- **Vercel Speed Insights**: Site performance measurement

You can refuse these cookies via the consent banner without affecting your browsing experience.

## Managing Your Preferences

You can modify your cookie preferences at any time:

- By deleting cookies from your browser settings
- By clearing the "cookieConsent" cookie to display the banner again
- By contacting our team at [contact.levestiaire.foot@gmail.com](mailto:contact.levestiaire.foot@gmail.com)

## Retention Period

- **Session cookies**: Deleted when the browser is closed
- **Consent cookie**: Kept until 2099 or until manual deletion
- **Analytics cookies**: 13 months maximum

## Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify and delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at [contact.levestiaire.foot@gmail.com](mailto:contact.levestiaire.foot@gmail.com)

## Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this cookie policy at any time. Changes will take effect upon publication on this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
