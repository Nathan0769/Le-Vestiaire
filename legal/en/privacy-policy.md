# Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: September 23, 2025

## 1. General Information

Le Vestiaire (accessible via le-vestiaire-foot.fr) is a web application for collecting football jerseys developed and operated by Nathan Rivollier. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## 2. Data Collected

### Registration Data

- Email address
- Username
- Profile picture (optional)

### Usage Data

- Jerseys added to your personal collection
- Jerseys added to your wishlist
- Personal notes on your jerseys
- Public jersey ratings (average ratings)
- Browsing data via Vercel Analytics

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

- **Your consent**: For registration and use of the service
- **Legitimate interest**: For service improvement and analytics

## 4. Use of Data

Your data is used to:

- Provide and maintain the collection service
- Manage your user account
- Calculate average jersey ratings (aggregated data)
- Improve user experience through analytics
- Contact you regarding your account if necessary

## 5. Data Sharing

### Public Data

- Your jersey ratings contribute to public average ratings
- Future feature: ability to share collections with friends

### Technical Service Providers

We share certain data with our service providers:

- **Google**: OAuth authentication (email, name, photo)
- **Vercel**: Hosting and analytics (browsing data)
- **Neon**: PostgreSQL database (all user data)
- **Supabase**: Profile image storage

## 6. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to countries outside the European Union through our service providers (mainly United States). These transfers are governed by standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions.

## 7. Retention Period

- **Active account**: Indefinite retention as long as your account exists
- **Google authentication**: Reconnection required every 14 days
- **Deletion**: Complete deletion upon your request

## 8. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Access**: Obtain a copy of your data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account
- **Portability**: Retrieve your data in a readable format
- **Objection**: Object to processing for legitimate reasons

**To exercise your rights, contact us at:** [contact.levestiaire.foot@gmail.com](mailto:contact.levestiaire.foot@gmail.com)

## 9. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including secure authentication and connection encryption.

## 10. Policy Changes

This policy may evolve. Significant changes will be notified to you by email or through the application.
